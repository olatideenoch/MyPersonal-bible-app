Install MyPersonal Bible

Get the app on your Android phone in four easy steps.

1
Download the App

Click the button below to download the APK file directly to your device.

Download APK

Size: ~3 MB • Version 1.0

2
Allow Installation

Android may block installation from unknown sources. Enable it temporarily:

Settings → Security → Install unknown apps
Find your browser (e.g., Chrome) and toggle “Allow from this source.”
3
Install the App

Open the downloaded file (usually from the notification bar or Downloads folder) and tap “Install.”

4
Open & Enjoy!

The MyPersonal Bible icon will appear on your home screen. Tap it to dive into God's Word anytime, anywhere.

This app works on Android 5.0 and above. No personal data is collected.
